# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Contributors are expected to keep project communication respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the software.

Examples of welcome behavior:

- Give and receive technical feedback in good faith.
- Explain tradeoffs clearly.
- Credit ideas and contributions fairly.
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Repeatedly derailing project discussions.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or private data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
